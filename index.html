Hello World...

Professors will be able to search the database by inputting their Social Security Number. Doing so will list the titles, classrooms, meeting days and time of his/her classes. Also, by inputting a course number and a section number the database will output how many students got a distinct grade, i.e. ‘A+,’ ‘A,’ ‘A-,‘ ‘B+,’ ‘B,’ ‘B-,‘ etc. These features will help professors stay organized during what can be a hectic school year.
Students will be able to search the database by inputting their course number. Doing so will list the sections of the course, including the classrooms, the meeting days and time, and the number of students enrolled in each section. Also, by inputting their campus-wide ID, a list of all courses the student has attempted and the grades. These features will help students find new courses to take as well as staying on top of grades.
